Case Study

extreme weather prediction for meghalaya state govt

3.5m lives. $75m in damages.

Meghalaya is home to the rainiest place in the world. It's also home to frequent and costly floods. Over the past 50 years in the northeast, an annual average of 3.5 million lives have been affected by floods, which have cost $75m in damages to agriculture and property.

From data to lives.

We manufactured $200 weather stations—from the circuit board, to the firmware, to the plastics, to the assembly. We combined our sensor data with AI to improve weather analytics. We provide policymakers a dashboard to send SMS alerts to at-risk zones.
