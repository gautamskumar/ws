Weather is local.

But emerging market weather data systems are not. Developed markets have 1 weather station / 50 km2. Emerging markets have 1 weather station / +10,000 km2. With insufficient ground-level data and large spatial averaging, satellite models vary up to 10C from actual temperatures.
